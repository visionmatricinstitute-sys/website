Electrical Formula Cheat Sheet

Vision Matrix Institute  |  Engineer's Toolkit

1. Ohm's Law

V = I × R   |   I = V / R   |   R = V / I V = voltage (volts), I = current (amps), R = resistance (ohms)

2. Power (DC / Resistive Circuits)

P = V × I   |   P = I² × R   |   P = V² / R

3. Single-Phase AC Power

P = V × I × cos(φ) cos(φ) = power factor

4. Three-Phase AC Power

P = √3 × VL × IL × cos(φ) VL, IL = line voltage and line current; √3 ≈ 1.732

5. Voltage Drop

Single-phase: Vd = 2 × I × R × L
Three-phase: Vd = √3 × I × R × L
%VD = (Vd / V) × 100 R in ohms/metre, L = one-way cable length in metres

6. Cable Short-Circuit Withstand (Adiabatic Equation)

Smin = (I × √t) / k Smin = minimum conductor CSA (mm²), I = fault current (A), t = clearance time (s)
